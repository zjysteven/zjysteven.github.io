I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student at Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Duke University, jointly supervised by Dr. Yiran Chen and Dr. Hai Li. I received my B.Eng. from Dept. of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University. My research interests lie in efficient and robust machine learning. More specifically, I'm focusing on improving the sparsity and robustness of deep neural networks.
